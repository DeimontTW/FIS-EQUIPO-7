# Functional Requirements (English Version)

1. The system needs to represent the infrastructure of the Faculty of Mathematics in a detailed 2D map and in a virtual Street View environment.  
2. Show and track the user’s location inside the faculty using the device’s GPS.  
3. Switch between the 2D map and the Street View with a single transition.  
4. Navigation controls represented by directional arrows (up, down, left, right).  
5. Legends and symbols of points of interest represented with colors.  
6. Send push notifications to the device when the cafeteria, school control, accounting, computer center, or library open or close, and when a teacher arrives or leaves their office.  
7. Search bar for points of interest.  
8. Route system with a start point and a destination point.  
9. Provide voice navigation instructions synthesized through native browser functions (turn or keep straight for a certain distance).  
10. The website text must be in Spanish and English.
